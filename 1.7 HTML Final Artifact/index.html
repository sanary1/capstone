Winter break Lake Tahoe recap

Over winter break I was able to celebrate my birthday and the new years in Lake Tahoe by skiing with my family and eating at Hell's Kitchen

Skiing at Heavenly

I went skiing at Heavenly with my family for my birthday. It was a lot of fun.



After skiing, we went to Hell's Kitchen for dinner to celebrate my birthday. The food was amazing and I had a great time with my family.

This is what we had to eat there!

  2. Beef Wellington

Although the drive back was long, it was a great trip to Lake Tahoe, and I can't wait to go back!
